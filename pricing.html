Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I start on FindLight as a Supplier?

You can begin by opening a free account, which permits you to upload up to 10 products without any charges. The registration less than a minute to complete. To start, simply follow this registration link.

How can I list products on FindLight?

After creating your account, you can list products via our user-friendly interface. Simply log in, navigate to the 'My Listings' section, and click 'Add Product'. Remember, with a free account, you can add up to 10 products.

How many products can I list under the Basic plan?

Under the Basic plan, you can list up to 10 products at no cost.

Can distributors join FindLight?

Absolutely! We welcome both original manufacturers and resellers, including distributors.

Does FindLight sell products directly?

No, FindLight does not sell products directly. We provide a platform for suppliers, manufacturers, and distributors to list and promote their products.

Does FindLight charge a transaction fee if a product is sold through the platform?

No, FindLight does not charge any transaction fees for products sold through our platform. We aim to facilitate connections between suppliers and customers without intervening in the sales process.

How does the "Sales Leads" feature work, and how is it different from the Basic plan to others?

The "Sales Leads" feature allows suppliers to receive inquiries from potential customers interested in their products. In the Basic plan, suppliers receive standard access to these leads. However, with premium tiers, due to promotional activities like premium placement, SEO optimization, and other advanced features, listed products have a higher likelihood of appearing in search results. As a result, premium tier subscribers often see increased visibility, which translates to receiving more qualified sales leads, thereby enhancing their lead activity and potential conversions.

How do I decide which pricing tier is best for my business?

Choosing the right pricing tier depends on your business needs and goals. Here's a guideline:

Basic Plan: Suitable for businesses looking to test the platform, offering up to 10 product listings.

Starter and Above: Ideal for businesses aiming for increased visibility and enhanced features. As you move up the tiers, you benefit from greater promotional activities, more product listings, premium placements, and additional marketing services.

We recommend assessing the volume of products you wish to list, the level of promotion you desire, and the additional features that align with your business strategy. If unsure, our support team is available to guide you based on your objectives and budget.

Can I switch between plans if my needs change?

Absolutely! You can easily upgrade or downgrade between plans as your business needs evolve. Just access your account settings and select the desired plan. Any changes in billing will be adjusted accordingly from the next billing cycle.

What benefits do I receive with the Starter or higher plans regarding product listings?

With the Starter plan and above, you receive:

  1. Increased Product Listings: More products can be listed compared to the Basic plan.
  2. SEO-Friendly Product Pages: Enhanced optimization to make your products more discoverable in search engines.
  3. Premium Placements: Your products get preferential spots, increasing their visibility to potential buyers.
  4. Backlinks: Boosting your site's SEO and directing more traffic to your pages.
  5. Custom Lead Forms: Tailored forms to capture lead information specific to your needs.

Additionally, as you move to even higher tiers, there are further benefits like Blog Post Sponsor Status, Preferential Lead Forwarding, and more. Each tier is designed to offer greater visibility and engagement for your listings.

What are the payment options for the different plans?

For all our plans, we accept major credit and debit cards. Depending on the chosen plan, you can opt for monthly or yearly billing. For select plans or bulk service purchases, we might also offer bank transfers or invoice-based payments.

Can I request a demo or trial before committing to a paid plan?

Certainly! While you can begin with our Basic plan to experience our platform, we also offer live demos via Zoom where a team member will walk you through the features and functionalities tailored to your interests. If you'd like to schedule a live demo, please contact us directly or choose an available timeslot on our Calendly below. We're here to assist and ensure you're making the most informed decision for your business.

Can I choose which products get the Premium Placement under paid plans?

Yes, you can! Under the paid plans that offer 'Premium Placement', you have the flexibility to select which of your products receive this enhanced visibility. Once you've chosen the products, they'll be given preferential spots on our platform, ensuring they catch the attention of potential buyers more effectively.

Can I select individual marketing services without subscribing to a full plan?

Yes, you can! We offer a range of individual marketing services, such as White Paper Production, Spotlight Articles, and Social Media Promotions, that can be availed separately without the need to commit to a full subscription plan. This allows you to tailor our services to your specific needs and budget. For pricing and further details on these standalone services, please refer to the "Marketing Services" section or contact our support team.

What support options are available for vendors?

For vendors, we offer comprehensive support including:

  • Email Support: For detailed queries or issues, you can reach out to our dedicated support team via email.
  • Knowledge Base: Access our online resources for common questions, tutorials, and best practices.
  • Live Demos: If you need hands-on assistance or a walkthrough, we offer live demos via Zoom to help you understand our platform better.
  • Dedicated Account Manager: For higher-tier plans, get personalized assistance and guidance on making the most of your FindLight experience.

Our aim is to ensure your success on our platform, and we're always here to help and guide you.
